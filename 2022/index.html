SwInBee 2022

The third SwInBee was held on Friday, 16 September, 2022.

Congratulations to Bill Owen, who was the overall winner!

Many thanks to Bill, Tien, Tabish, Laine, Josh, Praveen, and Michael for participating. The event was held in a really supportive atmosphere with a lot of enthusiasm for maths.

In the end the person named Champion Integrator was Bill who received a $200 prize, runner up was Tabish ($150), third place Praveen ($100), and fourth place Michael ($50).

You can download the pdf files for the qualifying round questions and questions with solutions. If you'd really like a worked solution for any of these integrals please contact Nathan.

The prize winners were:

Champion Integrator Bill Owen
Second prize Tabish Ali Rather
Third prize Praveen De Silva
Fourth prize Michael Tosin
