SwInBee 2023

The fourth SwInBee was held on Wednesday, 13 September, 2023.

Congratulations to Thanh An Nguyen, who was the overall winner!

Many thanks to An, Joe, Tabish, Bill, Liam, James, Thu, Mark, Duy, Andrew, Linh, and Daniel for participating. The event was held in a really supportive atmosphere with a lot of enthusiasm for maths.

In the end the person named Champion Integrator was An who received a $200 prize, runner up was Thu ($150), third place Tabish ($100), and fourth place Bill ($50). Both Tabish and Bill deserve a special mention as they backed up their prize-winning heroics from 2022 with further sterling efforts.

You can download the pdf files for the qualifying round questions and questions with solutions. If you'd really like a worked solution for any of these integrals please contact Nathan.

The prize winners were:

Champion Integrator Thanh An Nguyen
Second prize Minh Thu Nguyen
Third prize Tabish Ali Rather
Fourth prize William Owen
